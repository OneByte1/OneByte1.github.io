My Parliament

My Parliament enables users to keep a close eye on their democracy by allowing them to receive push notifications about members' activities and the progress of bills within the UK Parliament.

  • Get notified when an MP or Lord votes or contributes to Parliament.
  • Find out about their parliamentary history and contact information, both in the House of Commons and House of Lords.
  • Discover what members have voted for, spoke about in debates and advocated for in early day motions.
  • Track the progress of bills and get notified when they advance.
  • Find your local MP from your current location.
  • Bookmark members and bills to your dashboard for easy access.
  • Is white too bright? Enable dark mode.

Find your local MP, learn about their past record and keep track of what they are doing for you. Available now on Google Play!
